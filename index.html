About Me

Tha' Hitt Hut is run by Founder, Owner/ Operator Mark A. Johns A.K.A. (MJ Beatz).

A veteran professional, Musician, Producer, Audio Engineer, and Teaching Artist for The Philadelphia Symphony Orchestra. Mark is a highly skilled Music Production & Engineering seasoned professional that brings a dynamic blend of musical expertise, teaching experience, and industry knowledge.

A multi-instrumentalist proficient on all percussion instruments, including piano, with a seasoned formal education in music. Deep understanding of analog hardware, plugin hardware emulation, and comprehensive knowledge of the music industry. Accomplished writer, producer, and audio engineer for major recording artists and major record labels. Composer and MIDI Hardware Expert, consistently delivering musical excellence.
